Pretzel Eggs Recipe

Description

Great for kids! I used this to trick my best friends child into trying eggs! You can also add vegetables, cheese and meat.

Ingredients

  • 8 eggs beaten
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 ½ cups mini pretzels

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, melt butter over medium high heat
  2. Add eggs and pretzels to pan. Cook, stirring occasionally, until eggs are set
  3. Serve hot.

Nutrition Facts

Per Serving: 289 calories; protein 15.5g; carbohydrates 25.8g; fat 13.9g; cholesterol 379.6mg; sodium 702mg.
