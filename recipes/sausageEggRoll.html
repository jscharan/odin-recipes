Sausage Egg Roll Recipe

Description

Egg roll ingredients without the fried egg roll part.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground pork sausage
  • 6 cups coleslaw mix
  • 2 carrots, peeled and grated
  • Â¼ cup chopped green onions
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons shelled sunflower seeds (Optional)
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger root

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir pork sausage in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes.
  2. Add coleslaw mix, carrots, green onions, garlic, soy sauce, and sesame oil.
  3. Cook and stir until tender, 7 to 10 minutes
  4. Add ginger and sunflower seeds
  5. stir to combine.
